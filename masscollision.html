Mass Collision

is a "round"-based PvP Arena Brawl Game. You play a robot called "Maco" and want to push your opponents into a black hole!

The concept of the game was that all players give their inputs simultaneously, in one phase and the game acts them out in the next phase. Therefore every player has to consider the other players moves in advance to make a smart move. But the game has a very fast pace, so there is not much time to think.

I was mainly responsible for the concept and programming the game mechanics. We had a hard time managing the input of 2-4 controllers with the input manager of the free Unity version.

It was made during the Global Game Jam 2015 in 48hours. Here's the download!

Platform: Windows

Controls: you need at least two Controller to start the game.
