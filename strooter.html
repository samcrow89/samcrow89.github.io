Strooter

will be a mixture out of classical strategy and egoshooter set in hell. It's a project mainly made by Tjade Heilscher and me. We worked on the concept and the programming together. The 3D models you see below are mainly from the Unity Asset Store and turbosquid.com, the textures are from cgtextures.com and the sounds from soundbible. All of them will be replaced with own Art ASAP.

In this game I programmed the A.I., which can be extended with a variety of behaviors and can handle up to 80 NPC without a drop of the framerate. Mass collision avoidence must still be improved. I also integrated a lane managing system, which is not yet very intuitive. Still it can handle your troops while you have fun in the first person view. I also made the physic-based damage system and the soul currency systems, which are both working but not balanced yet.

you can download a newer version here!

Planed release: Fall 2015

(unusual) Controls: Q: change camera | O,P: open skilltree | Mousewheel: change weapon

Clicking on the textured part of the base will open your unit menu
