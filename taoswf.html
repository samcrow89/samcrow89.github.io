The Adventures Of Sir Wayne Finley

This is the first game I ever worked on. All I did was the level design (not the art!) and name the hero ;-)

It's a simple jump'n'run with 2 looped levels that repeat themselves while the levels crumble apart.

It was made during the GGJ2012 in Munich. You can download and play the game here!

Platform: Windows, Mac OS x+, Linux/unix
