Runaway

is a 3D Point'n'Click Adventure about a little boy who runs away from his babysitter to find his parents, which were going out for the night. The outcome of his future self depends on the experiences,which he makes that night. It was inspired by the two quotes above.

Here again I was responsable for the level design. Because we had no modeller, I decided we just use the planes out of the Unity standard assets. To make a 3D game out of the planes, I made imposters out of them which allows you to distinguish them from clickable objects (they always face to you).

It was made during the Molyjam 2013 in 48hours. Here's the download!

Platform: Windows

Controls: you have to hold the right mouse button to look around. Move with WASD. Jump with space.
